5 Common Phone Problems and How to Fix Them

Posted on: August 8, 2025

1. Phone Not Charging

This is often caused by a faulty charging port, dirt inside the port, or a bad cable. First, clean the port gently using a soft brush...

2. Slow Phone

Clear background apps, uninstall heavy apps, and free up storage space. For Androids, try a cache clean via settings.

3. Cracked Screen

Always use a screen guard and casing. For cracked screens, bring the phone in for repair — it's best not to DIY this one.

4. Overheating

Keep your phone out of the sun and close background apps. If the battery is swollen, it needs replacing.

5. Apps Crashing

Clear app cache or reinstall the app. Make sure your system software is up to date.
